Neapolitan pizza

Crust full of air, simplicity and the amazing taste of pizza.

Neapolitan pizza, or pizza Napoletana, is a type of pizza that originated in Naples, Italy. This style of pizza is prepared with simple and fresh ingredients: a basic dough, raw tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and olive oil. No fancy toppings are allowed!

Pizza Capricciosa

Mushrooms, prosciutto and the art of the toppings.

Capricciosa got it's name after prosciutto cotto - typical Italian boiled ham of pork leg and it is used on Capricious (Capricciosa). Mozzarella cheese goes with prosciutto, hand in hand complimneting eachother in an explosive twister of taste.

Pesto pizza

Basil, olive oil and the willingness to experiment.

Pesto Pizza is the perfect delicious change from a regular Pizza Margherita, a creamy Pesto makes the perfect base, then topped with slices of fresh tomatoes and shredded mozzarella. Pizza night never tasted so good! Pesto is never cooked and is usually tossed with pasta or in this case, used as a base for a delicious Pizza.
